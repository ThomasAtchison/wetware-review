BioBrickTM Studio Mobile is an experimental integrated development environment (IDE) that facilitates review, annotation, design, and implementation of standard biological parts from non-standard DNA, RNA, and protein sequences.Â  Our core goals are to develop programming languages, information models, and communication/computational architectures that support the development and responsible use of standard biological parts.


BioBrickTM Studio Mobile is an open-source project being developed via a collaboration between the students of the Biotechnology class at Abraham Lincoln High School in San Francisco, CA and the Endy Lab in the Department of Biological Engineering at Stanford University, Stanford, CA.
